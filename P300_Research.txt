While
 
======PAGE=====
mathematically

The

In

This

Consequently,

of

to
 
15
 
seconds

of

For

(DS)

and

the

a

Probabilistic
 
Formulation
 
and
 
Kernel
 
Density
 
Estimation
 
A
 
Bayesian

framework
 
conceptualizes

P300

as
 
a
 
sequential,

The
 
algorithm
 
initiates
 
by
 
assigning
 
a
 
uniform
 
prior
 
probability
 
to
 
each
 
character
 
in
 
the
 
grid
 
(e.g.,
 
$1/36$
 
for
 
a
 
standard
 
6x6

Following
 
every

flash,
 
the
 
underlying
 
classification
 
algorithm
 
(such

SWLDA,
 
xDAWN,

EEGNet)

continuous

$x_i$,

represents

target

To

$p(x_i|H_1)$,
 
representing
 
the
 
likelihood

observing
 
the
 
score
 
given
 
that
 
the
 
target
 
was
 
indeed
 
flashed,
 
and

representing
 
the
 
likelihood
 
given
 
that
 
a
 
non-target
 
was
 
flashed.
 
The
 
posterior
 
probability
 
for
 
each
 
character
 
$C$
 
in

matrix
 
is

recursively
 
based
 
on
 
whether
 
the
 
character
 
was
 
included

the
 
recent
 
flash
 
subset
 
$S_i$.
 
If
 
the
 
character
 
was
 
flashed,
 
its
 
probability
 
is
 
updated
 
via:
 
$P(C
 
|
 
x_i)
 
\propto
 
p(x_i|H_1)
 
\times

|
 
x_i)
 
\propto
 
p(x_i|H_0)
 
\times
 
P(C
 
|
 
x_{i-1})$

or

confidence).
